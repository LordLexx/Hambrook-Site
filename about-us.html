About Us

  1. Specialist Care
    2.
Our focus is always on the people we care for. We understand that being at home is best for everyone. At The Oaks, we offer an alternative for those whose home-based support is no longer sufficient, providing individualized care to meet their specific needs.

Each of our care homes within Bracebridge Care Group serves as a central point for the local community, offering guidance and support while emphasizing our ‘home first’ philosophy. We cater to those who need specialized care and support, creating tailored care plans with and for them. This approach ensures exceptional care experiences for everyone who chooses to live with us, whether for long-term residency or respite care. We are CQC-registered to provide the Regulated Activity of ‘Personal Care with Nursing,’ which involves delivering personal care to those unable to care for themselves due to age, illness, or disability, in the place where they are living at the time.

Why Choose Us?

Our dedicated team of experienced professionals offers personalized care tailored to each resident’s unique needs, from specialized dementia care to comprehensive nursing support. With state-of-the-art amenities, engaging activities, and a strong sense of community, we strive to create a safe and comfortable living environment where everyone feels valued and respected.

Our holistic, person-centered approach ensures that every resident is treated with dignity and compassion, reflecting their individual preferences and needs. Choose us for a care home experience that prioritizes the well-being, dignity, and happiness of your loved ones.

Our Team

Our team at Hambrook Meadows is truly exceptional, comprising dedicated professionals who go above and beyond to provide the highest quality of care. Their expertise, compassion, and commitment to our residents’ well-being make them the best in the field. Each team member brings a unique set of skills and experiences, working collaboratively to create a nurturing and supportive environment.

Whether it’s delivering personalized care, engaging residents in meaningful activities, or simply offering a kind word and a smile, our team excels in every aspect. Their unwavering dedication ensures that our residents receive the best possible care, making our care home a place where everyone feels valued and cherished.
